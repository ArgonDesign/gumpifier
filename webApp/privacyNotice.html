Privacy Notice

  • The images you upload are used solely by you. We do not use them for training other AIs, for marketing, or anything else.
  • Each time someone uses the Gumpifier, images uploaded more than five days ago are deleted. So, your images don't hang around on our servers indefinitely.
  • Your image is stored using a unique ID for its name. This means it's almost impossible for someone else to access your photos.
